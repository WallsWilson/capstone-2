Our Mission

At The Body Shop, we believe in transparency and ensuring that our customers are informed every step of the way. When you trust us with your vehicle's repair, you can expect a seamless process designed to bring your car back to its pre-accident condition. Allow us to guide you through the journey of restoration, starting with a thorough assessment of the damage. Our experienced technicians will carefully inspect your vehicle, providing you with a detailed report and an accurate estimate. Once we have your approval, our skilled team will embark on the repair process, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and the highest quality materials. Throughout the process, we will keep you updated on the progress, answering any questions you may have and addressing your concerns promptly. Finally, we will meticulously inspect the repaired vehicle, ensuring it meets our stringent standards of quality and safety. Our goal is not just to repair your car, but to exceed your expectations, leaving you with a restored vehicle that looks and performs as good as new.

Contact Your Insurance
Initiate the repair process by promptly contacting your insurance company. File a claim and await instructions from their knowledgeable team. Typically, your insurance provider will provide you with an estimate and an initial payment to expedite the repairs.
Schedule Inspection
Schedule a convenient appointment with us for a thorough inspection of your vehicle. Please ensure you bring essential items such as your driver's license, current registration, estimate from your insurance company, insurance card, and the initial payment provided by your insurer. During this meeting, we will also arrange a drop-off appointment for your vehicle's repair.
Drop Off & Supplement
Deliver your vehicle to our capable hands for repairs. Our skilled technicians will carefully disassemble your vehicle, meticulously reviewing the damage and identifying any additional parts needed. Subsequently, we will revise the estimate accordingly and promptly submit it to your insurance company for their supplemental approval.
Body Work
Upon receiving the necessary approval from your insurance company, we will commence the restoration process. Our experts will diligently perform the required body work, skillfully repairing and restoring your vehicle's exterior. All approved repairs related to the accident will be executed with utmost precision.
Paint
Once the body work is complete, your vehicle will move into our specialized paint department. Here, we take meticulous care in masking and protecting all areas not requiring paint. Thorough cleaning ensures a flawless finish. With the help of our state-of-the-art spray booth, we guarantee an impeccable paint match, giving your vehicle a seamless, factory-like appearance.
Polish
After the paint has dried, we remove the masking and proceed to carefully polish your vehicle. This meticulous step ensures the absence of any imperfections, resulting in a flawless, glossy finish that will leave your vehicle looking as good as new.
Reassembly
In this stage, our skilled technicians reassemble your vehicle with great attention to detail. Components such as lights, bumper covers, and trim pieces are precisely fitted, ensuring a seamless integration. We take pride in our meticulous approach to reassembly, leaving no detail overlooked.
Quality Check
To ensure the utmost quality, our dedicated specialists conduct a thorough inspection. Their keen eyes meticulously assess every aspect of the repair process, verifying the correct execution of repairs, flawless paint finish, proper installation of components, and overall cleanliness of your vehicleâ€”leaving no room for compromise.
Delivery
With the repairs complete, we will promptly contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time. During this final step, we will review all the repairs performed, addressing any questions or concerns you may have. Once satisfied, you can settle any remaining deductible payment and drive away in your fully restored vehicle, our commitment to excellence shining through.
